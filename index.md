## What this app does

This is a companion iOS app for IoT devices such as smart timers, sensors, and controllers. Typical features include:

- Device onboarding (Wi-Fi provisioning)
- Remote control and scheduling
- Alerts and notifications (e.g., alarm activated, schedule ran, task completed)
- Optional cloud sync to access devices from multiple phones
- Firmware updates and diagnostics
